About Us

At WebREIT, we bring a unique combination with over 30 years of real estate investment experience and over 10 years of digital asset investment expertise. We aim to provide affordable access to tokenized real estate investment opportunities for both Traditional Finance (TradFi) and Decentralized Finance (DeFi) customers.

Our Chief Advisor, George Bouyat, has over 30 years of experience in both residential and retail real estate management. We pride ourselves on delivering honest, high-quality opportunities for all investors.
